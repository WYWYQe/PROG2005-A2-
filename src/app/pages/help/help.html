Help & Troubleshooting

Frequently Asked Questions

Why can’t I add a new item?

Please make sure all required fields are filled in correctly and the item ID is unique.

Why can’t I update an item?

Updates are performed by item name. Make sure the item name exists in the inventory.

Why can’t I delete an item?

Deletion also works by item name. Please ensure the item exists before deleting it.

Why is my search not returning results?

Check your spelling or try a shorter keyword. Filters may also affect the results.

How can I reset the inventory data?

If local storage is used, clearing browser storage will remove saved data and reset the system.

Input Rules

  • Quantity must be a valid non-negative number
  • Price must be a valid non-negative number
  • Item ID must be unique
  • Item name is required for update and delete operations
